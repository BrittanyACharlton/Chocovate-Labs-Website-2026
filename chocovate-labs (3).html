// What we do

A new foundation for chocolate manufacturing.

Chocovate Labs is a Canadian food technology company building a scalable cocoa-free chocolate system. We don't make chocolate bars we make the ingredient platform that manufacturers use to make theirs.

Our fat-structuring technology delivers the same snap, gloss, and melt profile as traditional chocolate, with predictable pricing and a supply chain that isn't at the mercy of climate or conflict.
